Manufacturer of Manganese and Iron minerals and chemicals

Siddharth Industries was started by Mr Raghu Khemka (BTech Chemical) in the year 1995. A young man with a keen scientific acumen, Mr Raghu Khemka chose to leave the relatively cushy work as the manager of family owned automotive dealership. Having completed his MBA, he began his own venture in the chemical industry by chipping in his savings. Leveraging on manganese and iron ore reserves of India, he saw an opportunity to develop various value added products. His journey took him across India, finding best raw material sources, exploring various import substitute products and ultimately manufacturing quality products. Today, Siddharth Industries is a leading supplier of Magnetite Powder, Manganese Dioxide Powder, Manganous Oxide Powder etc. Siddharth Industries has a fully equipped manufacturing facility located in India. Our infrastructure includes:

1. Grinding Mills
2. Ore benefication equipment
3. Kilns, furnaces and dryers
4. Chemical reactors, vessels, reaction tanks
5. R&D and quality control lab for all raw material and product testings
6. Expert and experienced workforce
7. Warehouse and storage sheds
8. Distributor network and direct sales team.

We are a medium scale enterprise with ability to quickly develop solutions and deliver quality products with zero tolerance for red-tapeism.
